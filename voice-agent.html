Summary

Never Miss a Lead Again

For a litigation firm like Barton Cerjak, every inbound call is a potential client — and every missed call or slow response is an opportunity handed to a competitor. The reality of running a lean practice is that attorneys and staff can't always pick up the phone, and after-hours inquiries often go unanswered until it's too late.

This engagement deploys an AI-powered intake voice agent that works both directions. On the inbound side, it answers calls around the clock, guides prospects through a structured intake conversation, and qualifies them against the firm's practice areas. On the outbound side, it follows up with leads who submitted a form, left a voicemail, or went cold — keeping the firm top of mind without tying up staff time. Every qualified interaction automatically creates a record in Clio, ready for the team to act on. The pipeline runs even when the office doesn't.

How It Works

The Lead Flow

A prospect calls the firm

The voice agent answers instantly — any time of day, any day of the week. The caller hears a professional, on-brand greeting and is guided into the intake process.

The agent conducts intake

The agent asks structured questions about the nature of the matter, timeline, and relevant details — mirroring what a skilled intake coordinator would cover, without putting a human on call 24/7.

The lead is qualified and routed

Based on the conversation, the agent determines fit against the firm's practice areas. Qualified leads are flagged for follow-up; unqualified calls are handled gracefully with a referral or message.

The agent follows up outbound

For leads who submitted a form, left a voicemail, or went cold, the agent places a follow-up call — re-engaging the prospect and continuing the intake process without any staff involvement.

A Clio record is created automatically

Contact details and intake notes are pushed directly into Clio — no manual data entry. The team reviews a complete, organized lead record and can follow up immediately.
