Summary

AI as a Business Advantage for Barton Cerjak

Barton Cerjak Law is a lean, high-performing litigation firm competing in a demanding Milwaukee market. Like many boutique practices, the firm carries real operational weight — managing an active caseload, staying visible to clients and referral partners, and running a business — often with the same people doing all three. The opportunity is to put AI to work as a force multiplier: doing the heavy lifting on insight, research, and routine output so the team can stay focused on what only attorneys can do.

This engagement is built around that vision. Rather than adding headcount or layering in disconnected tools, Barton Cerjak is investing in a purpose-built AI system that learns the firm's context — its clients, its matters, its voice — and surfaces what to do next. The result is a practice that can grow its capability and its business without growing its overhead. Smart, sustainable, and built to compound over time.
